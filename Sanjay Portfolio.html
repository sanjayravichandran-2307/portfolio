Sealring Bonding Defect Investigation (8D)
Problem
Recurring sealring adhesive bond failures causing warranty risk and rework cost on the MBP Compact line.
Approach
  • Structured 8D investigation — Trials 1 & 2 ruled out adhesive shelf life and application pressure
  • Trial 3 introduced pin-type moisture meter to measure water bed moisture levels
  • Root cause confirmed: uncontrolled moisture in bonding water bed causing inconsistent adhesive cure
Outcome
Pin-type moisture meter added as mandatory process control. Defect rate dropped to zero post-implementation.
NDT System — Replacing Destructive Pull Tests
Problem

Approach
  • Evaluated Olympus BondMaster 1000e+ (MIA) for adhesive bond quality testing
  • Evaluated Olympus EPOCH 650 with CL5 probe (UT) for liner seam weld inspection
  • Built full ROI model: capital cost vs. sample savings + rework elimination
Outcome
Projected ₹75K–1.5L/month savings. 8–16 month payback. Recommendation submitted for capital approval.

GSM Variance Root Cause Analysis
Problem
Monthly WIP variance report showed unexplained material cost overruns not attributed to any known process parameter.
Approach
  • Pulled incoming inspection data for media paper across 6 months
  • Found consistent GSM readings of 115–118 vs. BOM specification of 114
  • Statistical analysis showed this variable explained 54–78% of monthly WIP variance
Outcome
Supplier corrective action raised. BOM spec updated with tolerance band. Dashboard adopted as standard monthly review tool.
